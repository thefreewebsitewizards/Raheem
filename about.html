ABOUT

SOLO LEVELING

Solo Leveling (나 혼자만 레벨업) is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. It was serialized in Kakao's digital platform KakaoPage and later published by D&C Media under their Papyrus label.

The story follows Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter in all of mankind, as he embarks on a quest to become the strongest, facing unimaginable dangers and uncovering the secrets of the dungeons and the source of his powers.

This fan website is dedicated to celebrating the incredible world of Solo Leveling, its characters, and its story. We aim to provide a comprehensive resource for fans to explore and enjoy the series.

THE WORLD OF HUNTERS

In a world where mysterious portals called "gates" appear, connecting our world to dungeons filled with monsters, certain humans have awakened to special abilities that allow them to hunt these monsters. These individuals are known as "hunters."

Hunters are ranked from E to S, with S being the strongest. Our protagonist, Sung Jin-Woo, starts as the weakest E-rank hunter, earning him the nickname "The World's Weakest Hunter."
